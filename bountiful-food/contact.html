Bountiful food's History

It was a long week on LA and the comunity was looking something to drink on july to enjoy their free time without work or education. Mark that is a brilliant chef was looking in LA to improve something new and fresh for anyone to enjoy.

Mark West starts to make fruit juices and share it with the people, but some of them do not like it. Mark was looking for other solutions to make better and listening the feedbacks from the comunity to improve his fruit juices. Finally, he realize with different selections the people would love their fruit juices.

Mark West build his own fruit juice company called Bountiful Food for anyone on July 1st, 2018.
